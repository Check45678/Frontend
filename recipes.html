This is a recipe on how to cook GARRI and ERU

Procedure

  1. slice water leaf and wash
  2. Wash eru and keep
  3. BOil meat, add canda and dry fish
  4. Into an empty pot, put your water leaf and allow to boil
  5. Add eru and stir continously to avoid burn
  6. Add your already boiled meat and continue stiring
  7. Add maggi and salt and taste
  8. Add your red oil and continue stiring
  9. Add crayfish and taste
  10. Stir again and allow to get ready
